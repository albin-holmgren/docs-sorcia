## Overview

Connect Confluence to search across spaces, pages, and attachments in your wiki.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect">
    Integrations → Confluence → "Connect"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Site">
    Provide your Confluence site URL
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Grant read permissions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Spaces">
    Choose which spaces to sync
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Synced Content

- Pages and blog posts
- Page comments
- Attached documents
- Page hierarchies

## Configuration

```yaml
Spaces:
  - Engineering
  - Product
  - Company Wiki

Exclude:
  - Personal Spaces
  - Archive
```

## Sync Time

- Small space (< 100 pages): 5 minutes
- Large space (> 1,000 pages): 20-30 minutes
