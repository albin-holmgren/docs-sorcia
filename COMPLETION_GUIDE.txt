2. **Overview** - 1-2 paragraphs
3. **Main Content** - Steps, code, or explanation
4. **Visual Elements** - At least 2 (callouts, tables, code blocks)

### Writing Style
- **Tone**: Professional, friendly, helpful
- **Format**: Short paragraphs, bullet points
- **Code**: Always show examples
